Competition Rules


Contest Rules

Eligibility & Essay Topics

Students from age groups 13-18 are welcome to enter! Participants can work in groups of up to 2 people. Submissions must be written and under 800 words.

Written aspects of each entry must have a title and be typed with 12 pt Times New Roman font. Essays can include graphics [such as graphs/statistics/images], which must be cited properly if found off the internet. See Competition Rules for more guidelines.
