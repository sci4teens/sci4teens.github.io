ABOUT

Sci4Teens is an organization that fosters an interest in STEM and science communication for teens globally through three key initiatives: contests, workshops, and a newsletter.

We opened submissions for our third writing contest. Currently, Sci4Teens hosts informative workshops and is leading the newsletter initiative!

Workshops
